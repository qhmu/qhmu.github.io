# Welcome to Quanhua Mu Lab!

We are a computational biology laboratory in the [Department of Applied Biology and Chemical Technology (ABCT)](https://www.polyu.edu.hk/abct/), the [Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU)](https://www.polyu.edu.hk/). Our research is centered on understanding **cancer evolution** using **multi-omics** and **artificial intelligence**, with a special focus on **sarcoma**. Check out our [Research](/research) and [Publications](/publications) for more information.
